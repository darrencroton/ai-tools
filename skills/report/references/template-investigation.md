# Investigation Template

Use for codebase exploration, behavioural analysis, execution tracing, or understanding how part of a system works.

## Required Additional Sections

- `Question Being Investigated`
- `System Areas Examined`
- `Observed Behaviour`
- `Evidence Trail`
- `Judgment Criteria` when the report makes recommendations or evaluative conclusions
- `Current Understanding`
- `Next Checks`

## Completion Gate

Do not finish until the report contains:

- the exact question being investigated
- the system areas examined
- the evidence trail that led to the current understanding
- `Judgment Criteria` before `Current Understanding` when the report makes recommendations or evaluative conclusions
- explicit unknowns or next checks where certainty is incomplete

## Template

```md
## Objective

## Scope

## Context

## Question Being Investigated

## System Areas Examined

## Method

## Evidence

## Observed Behaviour

## Evidence Trail

## Findings

## Judgment Criteria

## Current Understanding

## Risks / Unknowns

## Next Checks

## Recommended Next Actions

## Completion Status
```

## Notes

- Optimize for traceability, not polished prose.
- Show how the evidence connects to the conclusion.
- If you cannot confirm behaviour directly, say what supports the inference.
- If the report is purely descriptive, `Judgment Criteria` may be omitted or marked `Not applicable`.

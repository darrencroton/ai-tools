# Final Report Template

Use for close-out summaries, end-of-task assessments, or final delivery reports.

## Required Additional Sections

- `Requested Outcome`
- `Work Completed`
- `Judgment Criteria` when the report makes recommendations or evaluative conclusions
- `Validation Performed`
- `Outstanding Issues`
- `Residual Risks`
- `Recommended Follow-Up`

## Completion Gate

Do not finish until the report contains:

- the requested outcome
- what was completed
- `Judgment Criteria` before evaluative conclusions when the report makes recommendations or assessments
- what validation was or was not performed
- outstanding issues or an explicit statement that none remain
- residual risks

## Template

```md
## Objective

## Scope

## Context

## Requested Outcome

## Work Completed

## Method

## Evidence

## Findings

## Judgment Criteria

## Validation Performed

## Outstanding Issues

## Residual Risks

## Recommended Follow-Up

## Recommended Next Actions

## Completion Status
```

## Notes

- Use this mode when the main value is the close-out assessment, not the discovery process.
- Validation can be "not run" if true, but it must be stated explicitly.
- If the report is purely descriptive and does not make recommendations or evaluative conclusions, `Judgment Criteria` may be omitted or marked `Not applicable`.
